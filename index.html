About MozCode

Gear up for the year’s most awaited online Coding Competition! MozCode is a mega two-day event organised by MFC-VIT, which is a competitive coding competition hosted to test the coding skills of the participants.

So, Get ready for the brain game and test your problem-solving skills thereby intensifying your analytical and rational thinking. If you are a technophile and have the zeal to make a difference, here is your chance to showcase your tech-prowess in this coding competition.

About MFC-VIT

Mozilla Firefox Club VIT, one of the largest developers' communities in VIT, Vellore has been working with an aspiration of changing ideas into reality ever since its inception.

We provide a morale boosting system for the talented youth through our professional endeavors and inspire each student at VIT and beyond to folow our academic interests and goals. Members have immense dedication and devotion towards work, and toil very hard with a "never say no" spirit to achieve a singular goal of excellence in the field of technology with insuppressible zeal.
